My name is Filip Vuskovic and I am a 19 year old student from Serbia. I have dabbled in photography for quite a few years now and I have been keeping it around mostly as a hobby. Having said that, I hope to exapnd on that hobby and perhaps work on my skills some more and focus on turning this pleasure into something that I can one day make a living out of, or at least a side job that I can rely on from time to time. The beauty, that I personally, find in photography is the ability to capture moments, freeze them in an instant and preserve them for the rest of our days.

I am currently studying Internet Technologies on ICT School of Vocational Studies in Belgrade. My love for photography is shared with the love of computers and technology in general. Through Web Design, that I have been learning for over 6 months now and I find it very interesting and engaging to put those two together and create websites with my own work. This is the first project of that kind and all of the photos are taken by me and are my property.
